What started as a simple bootcamp assignment, Kampany.nl ended up into a live site where I can express my experience from what I’ve learned in three months. With the help of my colleagues, I’ve learned the first steps of being a front-end developer.

So how did this journey start anyway? I was working for two years as a customer-service employee at Teleperformance where I had to help KPN customers with their financial or technical difficulties. While I really loved to help people, the calls which I prefered the most are the technical ones. A lot of people call because they can't get their network operational or because they can't open websites for some reason. And it's not only the elder who are facing these difficulties, even the young are having difficulties to solve the problems themselves. Even though KPN does have an information page on how to adress the problems, they mostly say it's hard to find it on the website. This would mean that the website isn't userfriendly.
